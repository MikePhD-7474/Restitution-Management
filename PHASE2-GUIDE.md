## ✨ NEW FEATURES

### 1. 📊 Interactive Analytics Dashboard
**Location**: Analytics Tab

**What you get:**
- **Church Growth Chart** - See how your membership has grown over the last 12 months

1. Click "Analytics" tab

---

### 2. 💵 Petty Cash Management
**Location**: Petty Cash Tab

**What it does:**
- Track small cash expenses
- Add funds to petty cash
- See running balance
- Complete transaction history

**How to use:**
1. Click "Petty Cash" tab
2. Click "+ Add Transaction"
3. Select "Add Funds" or "Spend"
4. Enter amount and description
5. See running balance update automatically

**Example:**
- Add Funds: K 500 (for refreshments)
- Spend: K 50 (bought tea)
- Balance shows: K 450

---

**A) Income & Expenditure Statement**

4. Print or screenshot for records

## 📊 Understanding the Charts

### Dashboard Chart (Line Chart)
- **Green line** = Giving
- **Red line** = Expenses
- **Goal**: Green should be above red!

### Growth Chart (Line Chart)
- Shows cumulative member count
- **Steep upward** = fast growth
- **Flat** = no new members (time to evangelize!)

### Giving Trends (Bar Chart)
- One bar per month
- **Tall bars** = good giving month
- **Short bars** = low giving month
- Look for patterns (e.g., low in December?)

### Expense Breakdown (Pie Chart)
- Each slice = expense category
- **Big slice** = where most money goes
- Helps identify if spending is balanced

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to re-enter my old data?**
A: No! If you've been using the previous version with Firebase, your data syncs automatically.

**Q: Can I still use it offline?**
A: Yes! Works offline, syncs when you get internet.

**Q: Will charts work on my phone?**
A: Yes! All charts are responsive and work great on mobile.

**Q: Can I print the reports?**
A: Yes! Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P) on the Reports tab.

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: Your data is in Firebase (cloud) + localStorage (device). Both are backups!
